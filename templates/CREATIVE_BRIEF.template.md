# Creative Brief

## Goal
What should the viewer do or believe after watching?

## Audience
Who is watching, and what do they already care about?

## Platform
Target platform, aspect ratio, resolution, and any platform-specific constraints.

## Duration
Target duration and frame rate.

## Product / Offer
What is being promoted? What is the core value?

## Proof
Concrete facts, product details, metrics, screenshots, customer proof, or demos that may be shown. Avoid unsupported claims.

## Tone
Examples: Apple-like, premium, technical, high-energy social, cinematic, founder-led, warm human, luxury.

## Required Assets
Logo, screenshots, footage, audio, music, font files, brand colors, product URLs, legal copy.

## CTA
The final action or message.

## Hard Constraints
Required text, forbidden claims, language, accessibility, legal requirements, brand rules.

## Assumptions
List any production assumptions made because the user did not specify them.
