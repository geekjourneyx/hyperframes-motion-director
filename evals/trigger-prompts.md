# Trigger Prompt Set

Use these prompts later for trigger and non-trigger evaluation of the skill description.

## Should Trigger

1. "Use HyperFrames to create a 10 second Apple-style launch video for our AI app, with big typography, a product reveal, music hits, and a final CTA."
2. "Make this landing page into a 15 second YouTube promo. I want it to feel premium, not like a template."
3. "I need a kinetic typography ad with GSAP, typewriter text, gradient words, and a clean transition into the logo."
4. "Can you direct a short product reveal video in HyperFrames and give me the storyboard, beat map, snapshots, and render checklist?"
5. "The HyperFrames render works but the ad feels cheap. Help me improve the typography, motion, transitions, and review process."
6. "Build a 9:16 social ad from this SaaS copy using HTML/CSS motion graphics and make sure it validates before render."
7. "Turn our product screenshots into a launch-film style motion sequence with restrained Apple keynote pacing."
8. "I want a music-synced YouTube ad with bold text hits and a CTA, rendered from HTML and GSAP."
9. "Review my HyperFrames ad project and only change the title timing and CTA layout without rewriting the whole thing."
10. "Create a video production workflow skill for polished HyperFrames ads."
11. "Start a real HyperFrames project folder for a premium launch ad, including storyboard, beat map, motion map, snapshots folder, renders folder, and review report."
12. "I have a rendered HTML/GSAP video but it feels like a template. Diagnose typography, hero frames, transitions, and make a targeted edit plan."
13. "Build a review pack for this HyperFrames ad so the client can inspect snapshots, watch notes, blockers, and next edit."

## Should Not Trigger

1. "Write three YouTube ad scripts for my product, no video production needed."
2. "Explain the difference between Remotion and HyperFrames."
3. "Edit this existing MP4 file and remove the background noise."
4. "Create a static launch poster in HTML."
5. "Build a normal SaaS landing page with React and Tailwind."
6. "Generate a list of catchy CTA phrases for an ad campaign."
7. "Summarize HyperFrames documentation for me."
8. "Make a simple CSS typewriter effect for a website hero."
9. "Convert this Markdown article to a WeChat post."
10. "Design a brand color palette, no video or motion."
11. "Install HyperFrames and tell me whether it is better than Remotion, but don't make a video."
12. "Write a GSAP timeline for a website hero animation that will never be rendered as video."
13. "Make a project management checklist for a generic video shoot with cameras and actors."
